##  Alert Management Component

The Alert Management Component is responsible for generating and managing alerts when abnormal system conditions are detected.

### Responsibilities:
- Creating alerts when threshold violations occur  
- Storing alert details in the system  
- Maintaining alert status (active/resolved)  
- Tracking alert history for auditing and analysis  

This component ensures that system anomalies are properly recorded and managed throughout their lifecycle.

---

## Notification Component

The Notification Component ensures that users are informed when alerts are triggered.

### Responsibilities:
- Sending notifications to users when alerts are generated  
- Supporting communication channels such as Email or system notifications  
- Delivering notifications in real-time or near real-time  
- Ensuring reliable and consistent alert communication  

This component completes the monitoring workflow by ensuring that relevant stakeholders are promptly informed about system issues.
